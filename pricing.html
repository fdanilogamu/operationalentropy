Pricing questions

Why is the Hiring Archetype Map priced differently from everything else?

All other OEI engagements are scoped consulting work, so the price reflects time, complexity, and company size. The Hiring Archetype Map is a proprietary deliverable derived from your OEI results. It doesn't scale with revenue because you're not buying my hours, you're getting access to a built system that produces a specific output, and flat pricing reflects that.

What if I want to customize the scope?

Every engagement can be tailored to your specific needs. The prices above are starting points for standard engagements. Have a discovery call to discuss your situation and we'll scope it properly.

Can we do this remotely?

Yes. Interviews, workshops, and coaching all happen via video. Most engagements are fully remote. Some teams prefer an in-person kick-off. We can work that out in the discovery call.

What's the payment structure?

The diagnosis is paid upfront, as well as the one-year trend. The longer engagements (audit and executions sprints) are paid 50% up front, 50% at the midpoint.

What if we start with a diagnosis and decide we don't need more?

That's totally fine. The initial diagnosis delivers standalone value. You get a clear understanding of your organizational entropy, and you can decide what to do with that information. No pressure to continue.

Do you offer contracts?

Yes. For longer engagements, we provide a statement of work that outlines scope, timeline, deliverables, and investment. Everything is clear upfront.

What's not included in pricing?

Out-of-pocket expenses (travel, if in-person) are billed separately. Custom tool development, permanent staff augmentation, or extended implementations beyond the engagement window are out of scope.

Can you work with companies smaller than 20 people?

Yes, but the OEI's value increases with organization size and complexity. For very early-stage teams (under 10 people), you might not need a formal diagnosis, since operational entropy usually isn't the constraint yet. Have a discovery call and we'll be honest about whether it's the right fit.

Do you offer non-profits or discount pricing?

We work with non-profits and mission-driven organizations on a case-by-case basis. Reach out with your situation and we'll discuss options.
